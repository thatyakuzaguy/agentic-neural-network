# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include empathy, constructive technical
feedback, respect for different viewpoints, and accepting responsibility for
mistakes. Harassment, personal attacks, threats, sexualized content, and
publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may
temporarily or permanently ban participants whose conduct is inappropriate.
Report incidents privately through the repository's security or maintainer
contact channels. Maintainers will respect the reporter's privacy and respond
proportionately.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
