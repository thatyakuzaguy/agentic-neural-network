# Data Retention Policy Template

Legal and security review required before adoption.

## Retention Schedule

| Data Type | Default Retention | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Account data | Subscription term plus 90 days | Extend only where legally required |
| Product records | Customer-controlled | Support export and deletion |
| Audit logs | 1 year | Adjust for enterprise/compliance plans |
| Backups | 30 days | Encrypted, access controlled |
| Billing references | Statutory period | Avoid storing full card data |

## Deletion

Deletion requests must be tracked, approved, executed, and logged.

## Backups

Backups should expire according to policy and be restorable during the retention period.
