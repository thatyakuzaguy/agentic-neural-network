# Compliance

The compliance module is available through `/api/compliance` and the workbench compliance dashboard.

This material is not legal advice. GDPR, SOC2, ISO27001, accessibility, privacy, terms, DPA, and data-retention obligations require human legal and security review before production use.

## Covered Areas

- GDPR: data inventory, lawful basis, DSAR workflow, deletion/export, processor list.
- SOC2-lite: access controls, audit logs, change management, incident response, backup evidence.
- ISO27001-lite: asset inventory, risk register, policy ownership, supplier review.
- Accessibility: WCAG review, keyboard navigation, contrast, form labels, error messaging.
- Data retention: retention schedule, deletion job, backup retention, customer export.
- Consent/privacy: cookie policy, marketing consent, privacy policy, terms, DPA.

## Templates

Generated projects include starter templates for:

- Privacy policy
- Terms of service
- Data processing addendum
- Security policy
- Data retention policy

Root copies live in `docs/compliance-templates`.

These templates must be reviewed by qualified counsel.
